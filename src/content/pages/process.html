Our Steps

How to get started

Getting a website built shouldn't mean weeks of emails, confusing decisions, or chasing someone for updates. We've stripped the whole thing back to five straightforward steps - and most of our sites are live in under two weeks. Here's exactly how it works.

  • Step 1: Connect With Us

    Reach out and tell us about your business. We will get back to you to schedule a free consultation call.

  • Step 2: Design and Develop Your Website

    We get to work building your custom, hand-coded site tailored to your brand and goals. Most sites are fully built within 7 to 10 working days.

  • Step 3: Revisions and Feedback

    We share the finished site with you and work through any changes together. You get two full rounds of revisions before we go live. No rushing, no compromises.

  • Step 4: Launch Your Website

    Once you are happy, we go live. From your first call to a live website is typically less than 2 weeks. We handle every technical detail so you can focus on your business.

  • Step 5: Lifetime Maintenance

    Your site is looked after for as long as you are a Webber Creative client. Updates, edits, and support are all included. No extra charges, ever.
