About Us

The Story Behind Webber Creative

My name is Troy, and I am the founder and lead developer of Webber Creative, based in Canterbury, Kent.

With 5 years of professional software engineering experience, I have spent my career building high-performance digital products — and I started Webber Creative because I kept seeing the same problem: small businesses, tradespeople especially, either had no website at all or were stuck with something slow, outdated, and impossible to update.

Traditional agencies charge thousands upfront and disappear after launch. Page builders like Wix and WordPress leave you with bloated, slow sites that you have to manage yourself. Neither option works for a busy electrician, plumber, or decorator who just wants more calls coming in.

So I built a better model. Every Webber Creative site is hand-coded from scratch without templates or page builders, then bundled into one simple monthly fee that includes everything: design, development, hosting, updates, and support. You focus on your trade. I handle everything online.

Based in Kent and working with businesses across the UK, I take on a limited number of clients at a time to make sure every site gets the attention it deserves.
