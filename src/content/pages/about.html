About Us

The Story Behind Webber Creative

Who I am

My name is Troy, and I'm the founder of Webber Creative. I'm a full-stack software engineer in my late twenties, and I've spent the last five years building digital products professionally - first as a freelancer, then as a full-time engineer at a software company. I know how to build things that actually work, and I know what separates a website that performs from one that just exists.

Why I started this

Most web agencies charge thousands upfront, hand you something built on a bloated page builder, and disappear after launch. Small businesses - tradesmen especially - get the worst of it. They're busy. They don't have time to manage a website themselves, chase a developer for a text change, or figure out why their site loads slowly on a phone. They just want more calls coming in.

I started Webber Creative because that problem has a straightforward solution, and not enough people are offering it properly in the UK. Every site I build is hand-coded from scratch - no WordPress, no Wix, no templates - then bundled into one honest monthly fee that covers everything. You focus on your trade. I handle everything online.

What makes this different

I'm not an agency. There's no account manager passing your project between departments, no junior developer picking it up after the sale. When you work with Webber Creative, you work with me directly - from the first call to your site going live, and every update after that.

That means faster decisions, clearer communication, and a site built by someone who actually cares whether it works. I take on a limited number of clients at a time to make sure every project gets the attention it deserves.
